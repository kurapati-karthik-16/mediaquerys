Oreo Cake

Oreo cake is a dreamy, chocolatey delight with layers of cookies and cream in every bite!

Procedure

1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and grease a cake pan.

2. Mix dry ingredients: In a bowl, combine flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and sugar.

3. Add wet ingredients: Pour in milk, oil, vanilla extract, and warm water. Mix until smooth.

4. Fold in crushed Oreos gently.

5. Bake: Pour the batter into the cake pan and bake for 25 to 30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.

6. Cool the cake completely before frosting.

7. Prepare the frosting: Mix whipped cream with crushed Oreos. Spread it evenly over the cooled cake.

8. Decorate with whole Oreos on top and serve!
